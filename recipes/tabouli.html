Tabouli Salad

Return to Main Page

Fresh, Healthy, Cool and Savory

This gluten-free salad is a delightful combination of crunchy cucumbers, tangy tomatoes, sweet vine-ripened tomatoes and mint paired with the earth chewiness of the bulgar pearls .

Ingredients

  • 2 heads finely chopped curly parsley, soft stems included
  • 1 medium cucumber, deseeded and chopped
  • 3/4 cup finely chopped tomatoes
  • 1 cup cooked bulgar wheat pearls
  • 2 or 3 scallions finely chopped
  • 1/8 cup finely chopped fresh mint
  • lemon juice, olive oil and salt to taste

Steps

  1. Bring 1.5 cups water to boil, add bulgar, remove from heat, let cool
  2. Combine chopped parsley, cucumber, tomatoes, scallions and mint in a large bowl
  3. Add cooled bulgar wheat pearls
  4. Add plenty of olive oil, salt and lemon and mix well with a big spoon
  5. Salad should be light and bright. Add more lemon juice and salt if needed
  6. Refrigerate. This dish is best after sitting in fridge a few hours. Be sure to eat it up within 5 days
  7. Enjoy as a light snack or as a side with papadums and homemade hummus for a delicious gluten free meal
