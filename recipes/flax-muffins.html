Almond Meal Flax Muffins

Return to Main Page

Gluten free, Easy to digest and Deeelicious

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Almond Meal
  • 2 cups Flax Meal
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • salt
  • 1 tbsp cinnamon
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/8 cup coconut oil
  • 1/2 cup milk, half and half or water- approximate
  • 1 small banana, mashed - optional
  • 1 cup frozen blueberries
  • 1 tbsp vanilla

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350F
  2. Bring eggs to room temperature, melt coconut oil
  3. Add liquid ingredients, beat until smooth
  4. Add dry ingredients, adjust milk, water half and half to batter consistency
  5. Fold in 1 cup blueberries
  6. Fill muffin tins
  7. Add walnuts or chopped almonds on top of each muffin
  8. Bake at 350F for 25-30 minutes
    9. Let cool for 15 minutes before enjoying!
