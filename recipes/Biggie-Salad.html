Biggie Salad

Return to Main Page

It's a whole delicious meal

Add whatever makes you happy. Make sure the lettuce is crispy and fresh and that the dressing is savory and mouth watering

Here are my recommendations for the perfect Biggie Salad

Ingredients

  • Organic Romaine
  • Feta Goat Cheese
  • Nutritional Yeast
  • Scallions, chopped
  • Cottage Cheese
  • Walnuts and raw pumpkin seeds
  • Blueberries
  • Combination of fresh herbs like mint, basil, parsley, dill and thyme

Dressing

Olive Oil, Soy Sauce, Garlic Powder
